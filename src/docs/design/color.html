Color

Use colors purposefully to communicate purpose of the content and interface. This helps us create visual patterns that can make interacting with our websites easier and more predictable.

Primary colors

The University Libraries adheres to the University Relations color guidelines.

  • light maroon

  • gold

    c-secondary #ffcc33

Large font only

Large font is either a bold 19px (or greater), or a normal weight 24px or larger. WCAG 2 AAA ratio must be at least 4.5:1.

Brand requirements

Per University Relations and Libraries Communications agreement, all University Libraries’ affiliated websites are required to have both the University of Minnesota Driven to Discover wordmark as well as the M Libraries logo.

The logos may not be stretched or altered in any way.

View the University Relations branding guidelines for up-to-date information.

Header

Required
M favicon where technically feasible
University wordmark with Driven to Discover tagline
M Libraries unaltered logo
University global navigation

Summary

The header comprises two rows. The University navigation (University of Minnesota logo on the left with 3 links on the right) must be the first item, followed by the Libraries logo, with required minimum logo sizes.

Favicon

The favicon must have the M in either maroon or gold and passing contrast ratio requirements of 3:1 (7.5:1 or greater is preferred) for websites on the umn.edu domain.

University of Minnesota wordmark

Libraries are required to have the University of Minnesota wordmark, including the “Driven to Discover” tagline, in the upper left corner.

The wordmark must have the “registered” mark and may be an SVG file. The width — specifically of the “University of Minnesota” text in the logo — must be:

  • 220px and 12px margin around it for desktop sizes, and
  • 170px with 9px margin around it for smaller (mobile) sizes.

The University logo is recommended to have an official University color as the background, either maroon or gold.

UMN global navigation

The University of Minnesota links in the upper right corner:

  • “One Stop” link
  • “MyU” link (it may also be “MyU: For students, staff, and faculty”)
  • Search box
    The University Libraries have an exception that allows the search box to be a link to the search, to avoid conflict with other searches on the page

University Libraries logo

M Libraries logo immediately below the wordmark. The width (from the M to the end of Libraries) must be:

  • 260px and 10px margin around it for desktop sizes, and
  • 130px with 5px margin around it for smaller (mobile) sizes.
